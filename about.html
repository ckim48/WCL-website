About Women's Leadingship Conferece(WLC)

Anyone is capable of being a leader. Leadership is inclusive and if he or she has a spark to be the change. However in our current world, not everyone is invited. The focus of this Women’s Leadership Conference (WLC) is to discuss women’s leadership in modern society and to encourage and inspire attendees to take action. Change can start from subtle action that ripples to influence a greater community. Leadership does not always have to indicate government, but rather local and accessible. Connecting this premise to the social context of the post coronavirus pandemic, WLCis aimed to be an enlightening experience to all participants including the speaker and audience in becoming open and driven leaders.
