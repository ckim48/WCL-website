Theme of 2021

The importance of leadership and the valuing of all kinds of leaders.

This is an important difference from what many other women’s leadership conferences may advocate for. Oftentimes in presentations that endeavor to empower a certain group of people, another group of people are directly or indirectly put down. This is where the important distinction between equality and fairness comes in.

Our conference does not strive to achieve a perfect equality of the genders, nor a perfect equality amongst all members of society in terms of leadership ability. We believe that gender is not a factor that should influence our perspective on a person’s leadership ability.

In real life there are many instances where people do consider gender, which is less than ideal. What we can do, however, is educate the next generation and guide them towards the more mature and appropriate values and concepts.
