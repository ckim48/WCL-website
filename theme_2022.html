Theme of 2022

Deep dive into answering the question “What is leadership?"

The conference strives to enlighten highschool students on how leadership takes diverse forms in specific fields of study. This year, the conference focuses on two major fields of interest : STEM and Humanities.

As highschool students in an educational setting generously supportive of garnering leadership, students are sometimes prone to be ignorant of how the leadership learned in the classroom is actually applied in society and be informed of some challenges that may not be present within the highschool environment.

Entering high school, many students start inquiring about university and what they want to study or pursue as a career. As developing future leaders, having an opportunity to hear about how leadership takes form in the specific fields and other fields of study will be an insightful opportunity for participants to reflect on how their current leadership experiences may be directly applied or transformed in higher education and career.

The conference will also provide a critical opportunity for students to reflect on the fundamentals of leadership such as asking oneself the question ‘what is leadership?’. Allowing participants to take a step back from all their current leadership roles, the conference will support students in building their own unique leadership style, hearing many different perspectives, anecdotes and thoughts of current leaders in the ‘STEM’ and ‘Humanities’ careers.
